// 01about

NCad LLC is a Pennsylvania limited liability company based in Philadelphia, operated by a single founder — no investors, no growth-hacking, no dark patterns.

We design and build mobile applications end-to-end: product, design, engineering, and support. Every product we ship starts from a specific, local problem rather than a market opportunity — we'd rather build one thing well for the people who actually need it than build something broad and shallow.

Our first release, HITS, came out of a simple observation: Philadelphia has more live music on any given night than any single person can track, and no good way to find it. That's the kind of problem we like — narrow, real, and solvable with software that respects the people using it.

// 02products
live in philadelphia

HITS

A mobile app for discovering local live music — every gig posted directly by the artists and venues playing it, sorted by time and distance, with no algorithm deciding what you see. Artists get a free profile, a booking inbox, and simple analytics on who's actually finding their shows.
